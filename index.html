Features

Discover intuitive features all designed to enhance your experience

  • Sign Language Translation

    Instantly translates sign language gestures into text or speech, allowing users to communicate effortlessly with non-sign language speakers in real-time conversations.

  • Interpreter Booking

    Streamlines the process of booking certified sign language interpreters, enabling users to quickly schedule appointments based on their specific needs and interpreter availability.

  • Multilingual Support

    Offers translation and interpretation services across multiple sign languages, ensuring a wider range of users, including diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds.

  • Easy Navigation

    Provides a user-friendly interface with sign language video tutorials, making it easy for individuals to navigate the platform and access needed services through visual guidance.

  • Video Calling Integration

    Enables seamless virtual sign language interpretation via secure video calls, offering real-time communication between clients and interpreters, regardless of location.

  • Location-based Interpreter Hiring

    Allows users to search for interpreters based on geographic location, facilitating in-person bookings for individuals requiring face-to-face assistance.

Who We Are

We are a group of undergraduates passionate about empowering the deaf and non-verbal community. Our platform provides seamless sign language translation and interpreter services, ensuring accessibility, inclusivity, and efficient communication for all.

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to create a more inclusive world where communication barriers are removed, and every individual, regardless of their ability to speak or hear, can connect, learn, and thrive through accessible sign language translation and interpretation services.

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to empower the deaf and non-verbal community by providing seamless, efficient, and AI-driven sign language translation and interpretation services. We aim to connect users with certified interpreters, foster understanding, and promote inclusivity in all aspects of life.
